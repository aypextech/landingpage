Frequently asked questions

Do you build custom software or sell a product?
Both. We have our own CRM you can adopt and adapt, and we also design and engineer fully custom software, automations and tools around your specific process.
How long does a project take?
It depends on scope. A landing page can take days; an automation or a CRM rollout, a few weeks; a larger platform, longer. After a short discovery call we give you a clear timeline and milestones.
Do you work web and mobile?
Yes. We build responsive web apps and mobile experiences, and our automations work across both — so your users get the same quality wherever they are.
What about support after launch?
We don't disappear at go-live. We offer monitoring, maintenance and continuous improvement so your solution keeps evolving with your business.
How do we get started?
Send us a message with a few lines about what you need. We'll set up a short call, understand your goals, and come back with a proposal and next steps.
