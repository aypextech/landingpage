What it is

Coverage without writing the tests.

You give Khronos your product's context; it writes and runs the tests across web, mobile and API — and a QA engineer from our team validates the results.

How it works

Three steps. No setup on your side.

01

You share your product

Your documentation and a test login — or, for mobile, your app build. That's all we need to get started.

02

Khronos learns & builds

It maps your real flows and builds the tests — what the user does, what should happen, where it can break — covering the full journey end to end.

03

It runs & reports

The right tests run at the right time on every release. You get clear results — what passed, what failed, with evidence — reviewed by a human.
