What it is

Show it your product once. It learns it.

You don't have to dictate every test. You give Khronos your product's context — how it works, how people use it, what should happen at each step — and it understands your product and writes the tests for you.

Then it runs the right tests at the right time, on every release, and tells you in plain language what works and what broke — with a real QA engineer from Aypex watching over it.

How it works

Three steps. No setup on your side.

01

You share your product

Your documentation and a test login — or, for mobile, your app build. That's all we need to get started.

02

Khronos learns & builds

It maps your real flows and builds the tests — what the user does, what should happen, where it can break — covering the full journey end to end.

03

It runs & reports

The right tests run at the right time on every release. You get clear results — what passed, what failed, with evidence — reviewed by a human.
